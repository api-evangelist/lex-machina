For legal teams evaluating federal agency action, the Administrative Procedure Act, or APA, is a foundational statute. Often searched as “what is the administrative procedures act,” the APA establishes the framework for agency rulemaking, certain adjudications, public access to agency materials, and judicial review of agency decisions. June 2026 marks the 80th anniversary for the APA’s enactment in 1946.
